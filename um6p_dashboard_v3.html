UM6P averages 0.8 citations per patent against a typical ~1.5 for portfolios of similar age - a real but not alarming gap. Since citations take years to accumulate and most of these patents are under 4 years old, the more telling number is that 24% have already been cited at all. That impact is concentrated in materials/metallurgy and environmental technology - worth watching whether other areas catch up as they mature.
